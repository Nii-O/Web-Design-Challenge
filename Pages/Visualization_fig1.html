Max Temperature

In this plot we are looking at the relationship between temperature and latitude. We observe that the closer the latitude gets to "0" the higher the max temperature gets and the further away from "0" we get the lower the max temperature gets. This tells us that countries closer to the equator experience warmer weather than countries farther away from the equator
